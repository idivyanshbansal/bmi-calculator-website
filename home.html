BMI Information

BMI is a measure of a person's weight or overweight in terms of height and weight, and is intended to measure muscle mass. It is widely used as a general indicator of whether a person has a healthy body weight. Specifically, the value obtained in the BMI calculation is used to distinguish whether a person is underweight, underweight, obese, or obese, depending on the extent to which the distance falls.
